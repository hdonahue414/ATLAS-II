# Alumni and Current-Student Outcomes Layer

This layer tracks exact public examples of alumni/current students where available.

Purpose:
- identify what kind of documentary life each program appears to normalize
- distinguish prestige outcomes from sustainable/teaching/community outcomes
- ground trajectory forecasts in public examples

Caveats:
- public outcomes overrepresent visible/successful alumni
- silence/disappearance is harder to measure
- not all examples are equally verified
- current-student examples indicate culture, not guaranteed outcome

Pamunkey project remains exploratory/non-core and is not used as a primary admissions thesis anchor.
