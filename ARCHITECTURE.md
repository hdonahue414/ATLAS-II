# v6 Activation Layer

Every future system now exists as a school-specific module with:

- `score`: 0.0–1.0
- `confidence`: 0.0–1.0
- `status`: active_seeded or active_placeholder
- `notes`
- `evidence`

The dashboard includes:
- All Systems tab
- Mega Indices tab

Interpretation:
- High score + high confidence = robust evidence-supported strength
- High score + low confidence = promising but speculative
- Low score + high confidence = confirmed weakness/risk
- Low score + low confidence = unresolved concern

This prevents speculative future systems from masquerading as finished evidence.
